ConboNet: A Unified Content and Boundary Modeling Approach

for One-stage Wide-angle Image Correction

Wenting Luan, Author 2, Author 3
National University of Defense Technology (NUDT)
Paper Code

ConboNet is a novel approach for wide-angle image correction that unifies content and boundary modeling in a single stage. Our method effectively addresses the challenges of preserving both structural content and regular boundaries in wide-angle image correction, achieving superior results compared to traditional multi-stage approaches.
